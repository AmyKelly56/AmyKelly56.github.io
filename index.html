Mary Ellen Doyle

Mediator | Conflict Resolution Specialist

  • Twitter
  • Linkedin

Since 2015 Mary Ellen has been in the practice of mediation, organisational conflict management and conflict transformation. Mary Ellen employs a variety of methodologies and experiences to work with individuals to help them identify the root causes of their conflicts, thus helping to improve their self- awareness, emotional intelligence and conflict competence. She is a consultant of the Conflict Dynamics Profile (CDP) which is a workplace behavioural diagnostic tool as well as a certified CINERGY Conflict Management Coach. Her passion for conflict resolution and transformation extends larger than commercial and person-organisation conflicts to community mediation and peace building.

Mary Ellen holds an MA in Mediation and Conflict Intervention from the Edward M Kennedy Institute, Maynooth University (2015). She has experience mediating commercial disputes including over 30 hours for the Financial Service Ombudsman Bureau Ireland and over 100 hours of voluntary community mediations.

While working with the Edward M Kennedy Institute, Mary Ellen coordinated projects and events including; The Common Security and Defence Policy (CSDP) Mediation Training, The Annual Senator Edward M Kennedy Memorial Lecture: Peace and Integration - The need to unite against extremism and, The Annual Lieutenant General Dermot Early Memorial Lecture, Leadership within Society. In 2016 and 2017, in conjunction with the Irish Commercial Mediation Association (ICMA), Mary Ellen coordinated Mediation Awareness Week across the island of Ireland. She is the founder and director of the All- Ireland Young Mediator Competition (An initiative funded by Irish Commercial Mediation Association and The Law Society Ireland).

Mary Ellen is a steering committee and research member of the Kennedy Institute Workplace Mediation Research Group (KIWMRG). This group was founded in 2015 by a few researchers and practitioners to explore important questions that inform and support the continued development of workplace mediation in Ireland. In an effort to quantify the cost of conflict within organizations at various levels and also to evaluate and monitor their impact, Mary Ellen successfully developed a ‘cost of conflict calculator’ while working for the OAK Conflict Dynamics ltd. As the dedicated sole employee of the growing company OAK, Mary Ellen was highly motivated and thrived off the fast pace in which she had to work. Always making sure to plan and prioritise tasks as to meet all deadlines, she often had to work with many clients and different projects simultaneously.

Mary Ellen began 2017 in Kenya volunteering with the NGO Shalom Center for Conflict Resolution & Reconciliation (SCCRR) on their Peacebuilding workshops. For four months she worked on the strategic design and delivery of transformative programmes on electoral violence. From rural Marsabit and Samburu, to urban Nairobi and Nakuru counties, she worked with both victims and perpetrators of inter-ethnic violence. This work sparked a passion for Mary Ellen to expand her multidisciplinary approach of mediation and conflict management to peacebuilding. She is now co-leading the Mediators Beyond Borders International (MBBI) Kenya initiative.
